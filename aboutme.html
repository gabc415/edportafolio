About Me

Chemist and Chemistry Educator

Introduction

I am an experienced chemist and educator with a strong background in organic chemistry and a special interest in incorporating technology in education. I am working toward my Ph.D. in Chemistry at the University of Nevada, Reno.

My experiences in both research and teaching have provided me with a unique perspective regarding how technology can be used to enable student learning and involvement in chemistry.

Teaching Experience

I worked as a teaching assistant at the University of Nevada, Reno, and as a graduate instructor at San Francisco State University, instructing undergraduate chemistry labs and chemistry writing courses. My responsibilities included:

  • Creating lesson plans and incorporating them into active classrooms
  • Guiding students
  • Tracking students' progress through assignments and quizzes
  • Keeping very detailed records of the students' work

I am committed to creating a rich and diverse learning environment in which students can develop a solid foundation in chemistry and a passion for scientific investigation.

Research Experience

My PhD research has included research into anionic Elbs and Friedel-Crafts reactions for the synthesis of novel 2D polymers. I have employed cutting-edge techniques such as AFM, XRD, NMR, and STM to investigate the resultant structures, molecular architecture, surface topography, and electronic properties.

I also have experience in applying machine learning algorithms for materials modeling and data analysis to support more improved compound identification and synthesis processes.

LMM Training and Prompt Engineering

I possess experience in using my subject-matter knowledge to train deep learning models to accurately deliver chemistry and science content. Some of my past projects involved creating intricate prompts for large language models, discovering biases, intensifying testing, developing model reasoning, and reviewing outputs to increase model effectiveness.

Education

  • Master of Science (Physical Chemistry), San Francisco State University (2018)
  • Bachelor of Science (Biochemistry), San Francisco State University (2016)
